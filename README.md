## Local Development

1. [`nvm use`](https://github.com/nvm-sh/nvm)
1. `yarn install`
1. `yarn dev`
1. <http://localhost:3000/>

## For Vue

1. [`nvm use`](https://github.com/nvm-sh/nvm)
1. `yarn install`
1. `yarn dev:vue`
1. <http://localhost:3001/>

## For Angular

1. [`nvm use`](https://github.com/nvm-sh/nvm)
1. `yarn install`
1. `yarn build:angular`, or `yarn build:angular:watch` for live development
1. `yarn dev:angular`.
1. <http://localhost:4200/>
